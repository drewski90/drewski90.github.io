A Goddess’s Touch

Booking, payments, and operations webapp for a service business
A Goddess’s Touch Purpose Architecture

What Is A Goddess’s Touch?

A Goddess’s Touch is a full-stack commerce and operations platform built to replace manual scheduling, fragmented payments, and ad-hoc staff coordination.

The system centralizes bookings, online payments, staff scheduling, and affiliate referrals into a single real-time operational platform.

Who This Is For

Staff / Team Members

Manage availability, appointments, and daily schedules.

Customers

Book services and pay online in real time.

Affiliates

Share tracked referral links and generate bookings.

Core Workflows

Customer Booking
  • Customers browse services
  • Select time slots
  • Pay online via Square
Staff Scheduling
  • Set availability
  • Manage appointments
  • View daily schedules
Business Management
  • Owner manages pricing
  • Tracks revenue
  • Manages staff roles
Affiliate Referrals
  • Generate referral links
  • Track conversions
  • Attribute bookings

Platform Capabilities

Real-Time Booking Calendar

Customers book appointments in real time.

Online Payments

Integrated Square payment processing.

Staff Admin Dashboard

Team members manage schedules and appointments.

Appointment Management

Full CRUD appointment workflows.

Affiliate Referral System

Tracked referral links with attribution.

Role-Based Access

Permissions for owners, staff, and affiliates.
